Conference Dinner

The conference dinner takes place

Saturday 8.2, 19'30
Cafe Wortner
Wiedner Hauptstrasse 55

On this map you see the place marked with an orange D.

The restaurant is in walking distance from the university building (<15 minutes walk). If you prefer to use public transport, take any of the tram lines (tram numbers 1, 62, WLB) departing from in front of the university building to the stop Mayerhofgasse (when you exit the tram, the restaurant is already visible and just 100m further on and across the street).

All participants are invited. If you wish to bring accompanying persons, please contact us via the conference email as soon as possible.
